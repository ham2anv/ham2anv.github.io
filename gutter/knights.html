The ongoing collapse of the Alliance has given birth to a number of independent militant orders. Some are little more than powerful mercenary companies, but others adhere to a common code of honor that elevates them above thugs and warlords.

Disadvantages

Code of Honor (Star Knight)

-10 points

Regardless of your personal ambitions and the goals of your order, you follow the code of the star knight.

  • Bring no violence against the defenseless.
  • Personally maintain your arms and armor, or entrust their care only to your squire.
  • Provide comfortably for those in your service, to the limit of your means.
  • Wield no powered weapon against another knight unless they wield one against you.
  • Treat your word given to a fellow knight as sacred.
  • Accept a fellow knight's honorable surrender and render them to your order for ransom.

Equipment

Armor

Star knights typically wear commando battlesuits (p. UT183) with helmets. These suits often have the morphwear and rainbow features (p. UT189) and microbot arteries (p. UT189) filled with a paramedical crawler microbot swarm (p. UT201).

Weapons

Star knights wield the full array of firearms common to any soldier. In honorable combat with another knight, they typically wield a Superfine longsword (p. UT163 and MA227).
